The Week Ahead 9/17-9/23

Quick note: Even if you are not on the floor of a residential program, you can still join!

On the 17th, Intramural Sports Begin. See the Interesting Links (New Sidebar Feature!) to the right for more info. And head down to the National Constitutional Center any time to celebrate the Constitution's 225th anniversary.

On the 18th, at 6pm, come to the PDR for the first dinner discussion in our new series "When I Grow Up…" Listen to some amazing upperclassmen talk about their summer experiences while you eat catered food. At 7:30pm, head to Huntsman Hall to find out what the Vietnamese Students’ Association is all about in their first GBM of the year!

On the 19th, at 7pm, head to the Susquehanna Bank Center for a Kiss and Motley Crue concert. At 8pm, head to the Blue Lounge for Biosphere's first GBM this year.

On the 20th, at 12pm, head down to the Palestra for the Shoemaker Green Opening. Food and drinks will be provided. RSVP by the 17th. And at 8pm, head to the Blue Lounge for Perspectives in Humanities first GBM.

On the 21st, at 8pm, head to the Kimmel Center for a fusion of West African prayers, songs, and rhythms in the form of gospel, jazz, choral, and chamber music about the journey into slavery.

On the 23rd, at 2pm, head to the Colonial Theatre to see Out of the Past, a film noir with crime, seduction, betrayal, and vengeance
