Character of the Week

DIKSHA "DIXIE" BALIE

Hi, there! I'm Diksha (or Dixie, as you may call me) and I'm the EH2 RA. I'm studying South Asian Studies and OPIM - and really like Sanskrit. In my free time, you'll find me doing odd jobs, service, baking/cooking, cleaning, all in the name of training my mind to focus on important things. My goals in life include inner silence, service, and figuring out who I really am. I don't like waste, so from time to time you'll see me trying to put leftovers from house event to some use. If you see me doing so, come give me a hand. The more the merrier, I say.
