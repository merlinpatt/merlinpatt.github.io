Character of the Week

PILAR PADILLA

Hi Y’all! I’m KCECH’s Head House Manager, Pilar. I’m from TEXAS! I’m senior majoring in Communications, with a minor in Political Science. I also work for Communication Within the Curriculum here on campus and a non-profit arts organization called Art Sanctuary in south Philly. I dabble in freelance pro-bono life consulting (LAWL). And, my two pop culture loves are Star Wars and Beyonce. Honey badger is an accurate term to describe me. As you can see, I’m a little cray. Always willing to shoot the breeze about anything, find me on EH4!

The Week Ahead

The Week Ahead - September 10th to September 16th

On the 10th, at 7:30pm, head to World Cafe Live at 7:30pm for a story telling competition. And audition for Atma between 7pm and 11pm in Annenberg 209.

On the 11th, at 10am, head to the Sheraton for the first day of Career Link's Career Fair. And at 8pm, join WiCS for a workshop in web development. This event will help you get ready for next weekend's PennApps event!

On the 12th, head to the game room for a study break with food, and of course, games. The game room is located in the Kings Court half of KCECH on the side nearest the Sheraton and the WaWa. Unfortunately, the dinner discussion for that night has been cancelled.

On the 13th, at 8pm, see Antony and Cleopatra with PiH at the Penn Museum of Anthropology.

On the 14th, head to PennApps to see a two day hack-a-thon kick off at 6pm in Houston's Hall of Flags. At 7pm, see Owl City perform at the Theatre of Living Arts, At 9pm, check out an old style Vaudeville show at National Mechanics restaurant.

On the 15th, at 10:30am, meet up with BioSphere to join them on their canoe trip.

On the 16th, head to the Penn Museum again at 2:30pm to join Douglas Repetto in making your own walking tables.
